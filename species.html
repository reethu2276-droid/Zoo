Animal Species

Learn about the different species in our zoo

African Lion

Scientific Name: Panthera leo

Family: Felidae

Diet: Carnivore

Average Weight: 190 kg

Average Lifespan: 15 years

Habitat Type: Savanna

The king of beasts, African lions are iconic predators known for their magnificent manes and social pride structure.

African Elephant

Scientific Name: Loxodonta africana

Family: Elephantidae

Diet: Herbivore

Average Weight: 6,000 kg

Average Lifespan: 65 years

Habitat Type: Savanna

The largest land animals, African elephants are highly intelligent and play a crucial role in their ecosystems.

Bengal Tiger

Scientific Name: Panthera tigris tigris

Family: Felidae

Diet: Carnivore

Average Weight: 220 kg

Average Lifespan: 15 years

Habitat Type: Rainforest

Bengal tigers are powerful solitary hunters with distinctive orange coats and black stripes.

Giant Panda

Scientific Name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca

Family: Ursidae

Diet: Herbivore

Average Weight: 100 kg

Average Lifespan: 20 years

Habitat Type: Forest

Giant pandas are beloved symbols of conservation, spending most of their time eating bamboo in Chinese forests.

Red Kangaroo

Scientific Name: Macropus rufus

Family: Macropodidae

Diet: Herbivore

Average Weight: 85 kg

Average Lifespan: 18 years

Habitat Type: Grassland

Red kangaroos are the largest marsupials, known for their powerful hind legs and ability to hop at high speeds.
